Assignment 01 (Middle ages)

Completion requirements
To do: Make a submission
Opened: Monday, 5 December 2022, 1:00 AM
Due: Wednesday, 5 July 2023, 1:00 AM

Choose ONE of the following questions and reply in no more than 2000 words.


  1. "Mais où sont les neiges d'antan?" is one of the most famous lines in French literature and has ensured the lasting fame of De Villon. But what does it actually mean? Explain with reference to his works and at least one other author of the era.
  2. How do the Tristan versions of Marie de France (Chevrefoil), Béroul and Thomas compare and which, in your view, is the most memorable? Justify your opinion with quotations from at least two sources.
