Mount Orange is now running Moodle LMS 4.1!

by Anna Alexander - Saturday, December 3, 2022, 4:27 PM

We're delighted to announce that our school, Mount Orange, is running the latest version of Moodle, Moodle 4.1.

You can find out more in the 4.1 New Features documentation or watch the

Amongst other enhancements, this latest version includes UX improvements to the Database activity along with previewable presets. Try our the Database activity examples in our Activity examples courseto learn more.

Also, our teachers and students are welcome to try the new modern, accessible TinyMCE editor available from Preferences in your profile.

Other improvements such as extra options for custom reports and a new Grade summary report can be viewed by logging in with our manager or teacher accounts as usual. We hope you enjoy exploring Moodle LMS 4.1
